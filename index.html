how we work

01

safety first

we study alignment and responsible scaling before we need to. safety is the foundation of every model we train.

02

build in public

our training code and findings are published openly. knowledge shared grows the community faster.

03

global by design

the world speaks thousands of languages. we are closing the gap — building models for every person.

04

own the stack

we are not an api wrapper. our goal is to train proprietary models from scratch — owning everything.

from the founder

i started muxbite as a final-year bca student with no team, no funding, and no gpu cluster. what i do have is a clear conviction: that the most important ai models of the next decade will not be built in san francisco.

they will be built by people who believe intelligence should have no borders — no language barrier, no geography, no prerequisite of privilege. that is what muxbite is for.

shahnewaz alam
