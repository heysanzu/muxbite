How we work

01

Safety first

We study alignment and responsible scaling before we need to. Safety is not a feature we add later — it is the foundation of every model we train.

02

Build in public

Our training code, experiments, and findings are published openly. We believe the AI research community grows faster when knowledge is shared, not hoarded.

03

Global by design

The world speaks thousands of languages. Frontier AI speaks fewer than a hundred well. We are closing that gap — building models that work for every person, not just the most represented ones.

04

Own the stack

We are not an API wrapper. Our long-term goal is to train proprietary models from scratch — owning the architecture, the data, and the training pipeline.

From the founder

I started MUXBITE as a final-year BCA student with no team, no funding, and no GPU cluster. What I do have is a clear conviction: that the most important AI models of the next decade will not be built in San Francisco.

They will be built by people who believe intelligence should have no borders — no language barrier, no geography, no prerequisite of privilege. That is what MUXBITE is for.

Shahnewaz Alam
